Information

The two report services we offer.

  • Hotline


    Informantion About Hotline

    The Hotline report service aims at being a major part in the war against online crime . It’s main purpose is to provide a safer internet environment for everyone using the web. The operators that handle the reports are specially trained and well prepared and ready to act in order to prevent any online crime or to help a person that’s been a victim of online crime. Also, a cooperation between multiple countries has been established to further help such fenomena eclipse.

    The Hotline report service gives the ability to the person using it to submit an anonymous(if we wishes) report. After our operator validates the report, it will be forwarded to the police and be automatically deleted. You can file a report about the subjects listed below:

    • Child Pornography
    • Racicm / Xenophobia
    • Identity Theft(e.g. fake profile)
    • Hacking
    • Cyberbullying
    • Sexual Harassment
    • Grooming
    • Commersial Risks / Threats (e.g. Phishing)

  • Helpline


    Information About Helpline

    The Helpline report service aims at providing people the ability to discuss(anomymously if they wish) with trained professionals. Our trained professionals are people that can support and give psychological and technical advice to people about matters that concern online crime. Everyone has the ability to raise his concerns about harmful online behaviors or harmful online communication.

    Below is a list of subjects covered by the Helpline report service:


    • Child Pornography
    • Racicm / Xenophobia
    • Identity Theft (e.g. Fake Profile)
    • Hacking
    • Cyberbullying
    • Hacking
    • Sexual Harassment
    • Internet Addiction
    • Grooming
    • Spam / Unsolicited Contact From Strangers
    • Commercial Risks / Threats (e.g. Phishing)
