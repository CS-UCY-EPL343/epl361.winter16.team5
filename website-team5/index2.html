Below there is information about the two types of report.

  • Hotline


    About Hotline

    The main objective of Hotline is the fight against illegal content on the Internet in relation of child pornography and racism. It gives the opportunity to the public to denounce websites containing illegal content. It also helps the department Anti Cyber Crime in an effort to provide a safer internet for young and old people. Finally provides cooperation with international bodies and other complaints lines for coordination of all countries in the fight against this phenomenon. The phenomenon of violence in the internet may appear with the following ways:
    • Cyberbullying
    • Grooming
    • CSAM
    Child sexual abuse content
    • SAM
    Content Sexual exploitation
    • Racism/Xenophobia
    • Sexting

  • Helpline


    About Helpline

    Helpline aims to provide citizens of Cyprus means to speak anonymously to trained professionals who can support them psychologically, to any concern or threat may face over the internet. It offers the opportunity to the public to answer questions and express their concerns or queries on harmful online behavior, harmful communication and harmful content on the Internet. The line provides psychological and technical support. Also Helpline collaborate with the Helpline referring cases involving illegal content online..
    The Helpline deals with issues such as: • Cyberbullying
    • Change settings
    • Commercial risks/threats
    • Excessive use
    • Grooming
    • Hacking
    • Identity Thefts
    • Other potentially harmful content
    • Online reputation
    • Phishing
    • Privacy
    • Sexting
    • Sexual harassment
    • Spam
    • Technical settings
    • Unsolicited contact from strangers
